Battleship Online

Battleship is a strategy type guessing game for two players. It is played on ruled grids on which each player's fleet of warships are marked.
The locations of the fleets are concealed from the other player.
Players alternate turns firing at the other player's grid, and the objective of the game is to destroy the opposing player's fleet.
You can fire as many shots as the number of unsunken ships you currently have
The sonar function allows you to reveal a 3x3 area temporarily, if your submarine is intact
